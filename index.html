ESLOS ENANOS

BARCELONA SPAIN

The architectural intent for this house is to become an iconic beacon nestled within the landscape. It is situated on a prominent peak on the coast of Barcelona, which historically housed a defense tower that communicated signals of incoming ships to the Defells Castle.
